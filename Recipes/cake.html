Cake

Description

Indulge in the ultimate chocolate experience with this rich and moist KitKat cake! Covered in creamy chocolate frosting and surrounded by crunchy KitKat bars, it's the perfect treat for any occasion. Topped with delicious chocolate shavings, this cake is a chocoholic’s dream come true!

ingredients

3 cups of wheat flour
4 Eggs
1 cup of milk
4 tablespoons of chocolate powder
2 tablespoons of butter
1 can of condensed milk
4 Kitkat chocolate bars

Chocolate truffle

1 can of condensed milk
2 tablespoons of chocolate powder
1 tablespoon

Mode of Preparation

1- In a blender put the flour, eggs, milk, chocolate powder, butter and condensed milk.
2- Beat well until the dough reaches the uniform consistency and the mass becomes homogeneous.
3- Add the yeast and mix well for it to incorporate the dough.
4- Grease a shape with butter and chocolate powder.
5- Put the dough evenly.
6- Bake in preheated oven at 180º for 45 minutes.

Chocolate Covered

1- Place the pan over medium heat, add margarine, condensed milk and chocolate powder.
2- Stir well until it reaches the chocolate truffle point.
3- Take the cake from the oven and cover it completely with the chocolate truffle.
4- Around the cake put the kitkat standing and tie with a loop.
5- At the top of the cake put the M&M.
6- It is ready to serve.
7-The cake is amazing and the decoration is easy.
