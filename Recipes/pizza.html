Pizza

Pizza is a savory dish consisting of a flat, round base of dough topped with a tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings, such as mozzarella, basil, olives, onions, and pineapple. It is commonly topped with tomatoes, mozzarella, and a variety of cheeses, including cheddar, feta, or Parmesan.

Description

Enjoy the irresistible taste of homemade oven-baked pizza! With a golden crispy crust, flavorful tomato sauce, and melted cheese bubbling to perfection, every bite is pure delight. Customize with your favorite toppings and savor the taste of freshly baked goodness!

Ingredients for the Dough

1 egg;
1 cup of milk;
1 tablespoon of sugar;
2 cubes of biological yeast (15g each);
4 tablespoons of olive oil;
Half a kilogram of wheat flour;
A pinch of salt.

Preparation Method for the Dough

1. In a bowl, mix the yeast cubes with the sugar.
2. Add the egg, milk, and olive oil, then mix well.
3. Gradually add the wheat flour.
4. Finally, add the salt and mix until a homogeneous dough forms, slightly detaching from your hands.
5. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let it rise for about 30 minutes or until it doubles in volume.
6. Roll out 2 medium-thickness discs using a rolling pin on a floured surface, folding the edges to form the crust.
7. Place the dough on a 35 cm diameter baking tray greased with olive oil and pre-bake for 8 minutes in a preheated oven at 180¬∞C.
8. Finally, add the desired topping and return it to the oven to heat.
